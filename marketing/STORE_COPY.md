## Title

## Subtitle

## Short description (2–3 sentences)

Welcome to Brindal & Grayson Cow Ranch — a kid-friendly world template where every cow is part of the family. Tap the Ranch Bell to open barn menus, catch wild cows with the Feed Bag, and grow from a small pen to a legendary spread. Discover rare Spot and Storm coats, fill your trait catalog, and earn gold, emeralds, and diamonds as your ranch levels up.

## Feature bullets

- **Tap-to-play barn menus** — Ranch Bell and Feed Bag; no typing required for kids
- **Deploy your herd** — Switch active cows, see trait tags, size, and gold-horn shine
- **Catch & breed** — Feed Bag wild cows; unlock breeding at Yard rank (3+ cows)
- **Spot & Storm cows** — Rare coats with custom entities (Holiday Creator Features included)
- **Ranch ranks** — Pen → Yard → Ranch → Spread → Legend as your herd grows
- **Trait catalog & loot** — Discover coats and earn Minecraft rewards
- **Ready-to-play world** — Packs and experiments pre-linked; Beta APIs + HCF locked ON
